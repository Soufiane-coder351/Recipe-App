Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with creamy sauce. Delicious and comforting!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

Step 1

Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.

Step 2

Fry the pancetta until crispy.

Step 3

Beat the eggs and mix with grated pecorino cheese.

Step 4

Combine everything and mix well. Serve hot.
